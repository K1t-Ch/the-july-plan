Plan B

The other doors

Every serious alternative was researched. Naxos, Milos and the Athens Riviera didn't make the cut.

Paros instead

The original plan — flip the switch up top. Two nights of Naoussa calm (Avant Mar, Cosme, Oroséa) via Athens + ferry, and a 35-minute Sunday hop to Mykonos. Cheaper, chiller, less iconic.

Santorini instead

The postcard — flip the switch up top. Direct flight Friday, three caldera sunsets, and the 3-night stay clears every hotel minimum. Costs more, photographs better.

Tinos

The dark horse. Odera — new Autograph Collection, 54 pool suites, a third of Santorini's prices — and Mykonos is a 15-minute ferry away. Zero-stress option.

The Avios play

BA Mykonos runs 16,750 Avios + £1 each way per person in peak. 33,500 + £2 for two beats any summer cash fare — check reward space before paying cash for BA legs.
